About R-Infinity Club

Rinex.ai has launched an R-Infinity Club in our Deogiri Institute of Engineering and Management Studies. Rinex.ai is a learning platform with ahead time vision and growth specific learning module that believes in practical knowledge and mastering them.

  • Members have an opportunity to manage and work as a team to organize multiple technical and non-technical events.
  • Membership to the R-Infinity Club is free and always will be.
  • Members with specific roles will be awarded and will get mentorship from industry experts.

Event Categories

We are organizing various interesting events in both technical and non-technical categories.

COMPETITIVE PROGRAMMING

Competitive programming helps you learn how to effectively work together, as you must work with others on your team to complete the same task.

HACKATHON

A social coding event that brings computer programmer and other interested people together to build, improve and innovate solutions community.

PAPER PRESENTATION

Paper presentation involves the preparation of writing and then the presentation of that to a panel who have the opportunity to question the ideas in that paper.

PHOTOGRAPHY

Photography contests is a great way for photographers to get recognition and benchmark their work against peers from around the world.

SINGING

Event where talented singers display their skills and compete to win.

EXTEMPORE

Spoken, carried out, or composed with little or no preparation or forethought. An extempore speech.
